# References: Unit 6 — Quine-McCluskey Method

1. [Quine–McCluskey algorithm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quine%E2%80%93McCluskey_algorithm) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive coverage of the QM algorithm including tabular method, prime implicant identification, and minimum cover selection. Essential reference for systematic Boolean minimization.

2. [Petrick's method](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petrick%27s_method) - Wikipedia - Detailed explanation of Petrick's method for finding all minimum covers when cyclic prime implicant charts occur. Critical for handling cases where essential prime implicants don't cover all minterms.

3. [Boolean satisfiability problem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boolean_satisfiability_problem) - Wikipedia - Overview of computational complexity in Boolean optimization, providing context for why QM has exponential worst-case complexity and motivating heuristic approaches.

4. Digital Design (6th Edition) - M. Morris Mano, Michael D. Ciletti - Pearson - Chapter 4 includes QM coverage with step-by-step examples of the tabular method and prime implicant chart construction.

5. Switching and Finite Automata Theory (3rd Edition) - Zvi Kohavi, Niraj K. Jha - Cambridge University Press - Chapter 4 provides rigorous treatment of QM algorithm with proofs of optimality and complexity analysis.

6. [Quine-McCluskey Tutorial](https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/textbook/digital/chpt-8/quine-mccluskey-method/) - All About Circuits - Step-by-step walkthrough of the QM algorithm with worked examples showing grouping, combination, and prime implicant selection.

7. [QM Method Examples](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/quine-mccluskey-method/) - GeeksforGeeks - Multiple worked examples of QM algorithm including don't care handling and prime implicant chart construction with detailed explanations.

8. [Prime Implicant Charts](https://www.tutorialspoint.com/digital_circuits/digital_circuits_quine_mccluskey_tabular_method.htm) - TutorialsPoint - Tutorial covering prime implicant chart construction, essential PI identification, and row/column dominance techniques for chart reduction.

9. [QM vs K-map Comparison](https://www.elprocus.com/quine-mccluskey-method/) - ElProCus - Practical comparison of K-maps and QM method discussing when to use each approach based on number of variables and automation requirements.

10. [Boolean Expression Minimizer](https://www.mathematik.uni-marburg.de/~thormae/lectures/ti1/code/qmc/) - University of Marburg - Online QM algorithm implementation that shows all intermediate steps, useful for verifying manual calculations and understanding the algorithm's progression.
