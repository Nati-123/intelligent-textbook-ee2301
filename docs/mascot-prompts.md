# Mascot Design Prompts

Use these prompts with AI image generators (DALL-E, Midjourney, etc.) to create mascot candidates for the EE 2301 Digital System Design textbook.

---

## 1. Friendly Logic Gate Character

> A cute, friendly cartoon AND logic gate character with big expressive eyes, small arms and legs, rendered in a flat vector illustration style. The gate shape is rounded and approachable with a deep purple body and amber/gold highlights. The character is waving and has a small speech bubble showing "1 + 1 = 1". Clean white background, suitable for a textbook mascot. Kawaii-inspired, minimal shading, educational feel.

---

## 2. Chip the Microchip

> A friendly anthropomorphic microchip (integrated circuit) mascot in flat vector style. The rectangular chip body is deep purple with amber/gold pin-legs that act as feet. The top surface has a small LED screen face showing a happy expression. One hand holds a magnifying glass, the other gives a thumbs up. Circuit trace patterns decorate the body. Clean white background, modern educational illustration style, suitable as a textbook logo mascot.

---

## 3. Binary Bot

> A small, adorable robot mascot made of binary digits (0s and 1s) forming its body structure. The robot has glowing amber LED eyes and a deep purple metallic body. It has a rounded head with an antenna, stubby arms, and chunky feet. Tiny 0s and 1s float around it like sparkles. Flat vector illustration style, clean white background, friendly and approachable, suitable for a digital systems design textbook.

---

## 4. Goldy the Digital Gopher

> A cartoon golden gopher (University of Minnesota mascot style) wearing safety goggles pushed up on its forehead and a deep purple lab coat. The gopher is holding a breadboard with LEDs in one paw and a soldering iron in the other. It has a confident, friendly smile. Amber/gold fur with deep purple clothing accents. Flat vector illustration style, clean white background, educational and approachable, suitable as a university engineering course mascot.

---

## 5. Flip-Flop Fox

> A clever, curious cartoon fox mascot with deep purple fur accented by glowing amber circuit-trace patterns running along its tail, ears, and body. The fox is sitting and holding a flip-flop circuit diagram like a scroll. Its eyes are bright amber. The style is modern flat vector illustration with clean lines, minimal shading, and a clean white background. Friendly and intelligent expression, suitable as a digital electronics textbook mascot.

---

## Bonus: Combined — Digital Gopher with Chip

> A cartoon golden gopher (University of Minnesota mascot) wearing a deep purple engineer's vest with circuit trace patterns embroidered in amber/gold. The gopher proudly holds up a large friendly microchip character (with a tiny smiling face on the chip) in one paw. Safety goggles rest on its head. The gopher stands on a breadboard base. Flat vector illustration style, clean white background, warm and educational feel, suitable as a university digital systems design course mascot.
