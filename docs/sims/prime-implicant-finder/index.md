## Key Concepts

### Prime Implicant (PI)
A product term that cannot be combined with another to form a simpler term.

### Essential Prime Implicant (EPI)
A prime implicant that covers at least one minterm not covered by any other PI.

### Minimal Cover
The smallest set of PIs that covers all minterms.
