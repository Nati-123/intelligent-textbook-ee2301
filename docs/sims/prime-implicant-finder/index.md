<h2 style="color: #5A3EED !important; border-bottom: 2px solid #5A3EED; padding-bottom: 0.3rem; font-weight: 700; margin-top: 2rem;">Key Concepts</h2>

<div style="background: #FFF7DD; border: 2px solid #F0D87A; border-radius: 12px; padding: 20px 24px; margin: 1rem 0; box-shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(184,134,11,0.08);">
<p style="color: #B8860B; font-weight: 700; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 4px;">Prime Implicant (PI)</p>
<p style="color: #333; line-height: 1.75; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 1rem;">A product term that cannot be combined with another to form a simpler term.</p>
<p style="color: #B8860B; font-weight: 700; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 4px;">Essential Prime Implicant (EPI)</p>
<p style="color: #333; line-height: 1.75; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 1rem;">A prime implicant that covers at least one minterm not covered by any other PI.</p>
<p style="color: #B8860B; font-weight: 700; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 4px;">Minimal Cover</p>
<p style="color: #333; line-height: 1.75; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">The smallest set of PIs that covers all minterms.</p>
</div>
